About me

I was born on July 31st, 1980, to James and Lily Potter, who were both members of the Order of the Phoenix, a secret organization that fought against Voldemort and his followers. They sacrificed their lives to protect me from Voldemort’s curse, which left me with a lightning-shaped scar on my forehead. I grew up with my aunt and uncle, who were Muggles (non-magical people) and treated me very poorly. I didn’t know I was a wizard until I received a letter from Hogwarts when I was eleven years old.
